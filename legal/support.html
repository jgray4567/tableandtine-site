Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Table & Tine → Cancel Subscription. Your Premium features remain active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Open Table & Tine → Settings → Restore Purchases. Apple will verify your subscription automatically.

How do I export my recipes?

Open Table & Tine → Settings → Export My Cookbook. Choose PDF or JSON format.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email support@tableandtine.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots help!
